## Alternative: Simple Background Images

If you prefer simpler images, you can use:
- **Abstract gradients** with floating geometric shapes
- **Soft bokeh backgrounds** with colorful orbs
- **Magical particle effects** on solid color backgrounds
- **Illustrated patterns** with children's book elements

---

## Tips for Best Results:

1. **Use consistent style** across all images (same art style/technique)
2. **Match color schemes**: Purple, blue, cyan, pink gradients
3. **Leave space for text**: Images will have an 80% opacity gradient overlay
4. **Focus on center**: Important elements should be centered
5. **High contrast**: Ensure images work well with text overlay
